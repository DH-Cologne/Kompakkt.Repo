Kompakkt brings 3D models to the web and allows multimedia annotations.
It is a pleasure to support sharing of knowledge and open source software - not only for research purposes but for everyone.
explore

Explore. Share. Embed.

Kompakkt covers images, videos, audio files and 3D models. Explore them in 3D and become part of our community to share your own content. It is all here, in you browser. Furthermore, you can embed uploaded objects on other websites via iframe.
place

Annotate.

Interact with the Kompakkt objects by adding descriptions or references to specific points in their space. You can also interlink Kompakkt multimedia objects. Explore annotations of others or annotate together with others.
people

Collaborate

Objects, Annotations and the process of annotating allows collaboration with other Kompakkt users. Known from the whole Kompakkt community and family, life is mostly better with friends. Trusting them, Kompakkt loves to share, starting by its own source code.
